Program Objective/Description

As a Web Developer

  1. You will gain a wide array of fundamental and in-depth training on front end web development, as well as fundamentals of back end development.
  2. You will learn how to effectively write front-end programs which interact with servers and load asynchronously.
  3. You will also learn to design and implement graphical interfaces which follow best practices of UX design and are mobile-friendly.
  4. Your back-end experience will include an introduction to common design patterns.

Training Objective

To build an understanding of designing, creating, and maintaining websites through different programming languages.
